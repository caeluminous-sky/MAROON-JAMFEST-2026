CRITERIA FOR JUDGING

CRITERIA PERCENTAGE
Musicality (Harmony, Rhythm, Sound Quality) 60%
Performance (Stage Presence, Crowd Engagement, Energy, Confidence) 30%
Audience Impact (General Impression, Audience Response) 10%
TOTAL 100%

Musicality: Evaluates the overall musical elements, including how well the harmony, rhythm, and sound quality blend to create a cohesive and pleasing performance.

Performance: Assesses the performer's ability to command the stage, connect with the audience, display high energy, and exude confidence throughout the presentation.

Audience Impact: Measures the overall impression left on the audience, including their reactions, engagement, and how memorable the performance is.

PERFORMANCE GUIDELINES

1. Each band must perform three (3) full songs:

  • One OPM song
  • One R&B song
  • One song of their choice

2. The total performance time must not exceed 15 minutes, including transitions.

3. Medley and special arrangement is not allowed.

4. Lyrics and performances MUST NOT HAVE OFFENSIVE LANGUAGES.
